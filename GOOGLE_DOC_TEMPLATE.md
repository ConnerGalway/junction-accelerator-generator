# 90-DAY ROADMAP

> **How to fill in the Roadmap**
>
> Each week has two columns:
> - **Actions** — what the client needs to DO this week (instructions)
> - **Checklist** — what the client can tick off to confirm it's DONE (verifiable outcomes)
>
> **The → links to annotation** is the most important thing to get right.
> For any action bullet that has a corresponding numbered step in the tactics above, add:
> `→ links to: Tactic [1/2/3] / Step [N]`
> This creates the "how to" buttons on the webpage that take the client directly to the instructions.
> If an action has no matching tactic step, leave no annotation.
>
> Month names appear as section headers. Use whatever names fit the plan.
